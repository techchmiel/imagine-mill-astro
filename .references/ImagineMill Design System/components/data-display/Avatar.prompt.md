One-line: A circular author/user avatar — pass `src` for a photo or just `name` to render initials over the brand gradient.

```jsx
<Avatar name="Jordan Vale" src="/team/jordan.jpg" size="md" />
<Avatar name="Priya Shah" size="sm" ring />
```

Sizes: `xs` 24 / `sm` 32 / `md` 44 / `lg` 64px. `ring` adds the brand halo.
