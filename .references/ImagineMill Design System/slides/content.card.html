Audit checklist

Four things to lock down first

1

Sharing settings

Restrict tenant-wide external sharing to existing guests before any licenses are assigned.

2

Sensitivity labels

Confirm confidential content is labeled so Copilot respects the right boundaries.

3

Access reviews

Surface anything shared with "Everyone" and tidy up over-broad permissions.

4

Pilot group

Pick 15–20 people across three teams — not just the enthusiasts.
